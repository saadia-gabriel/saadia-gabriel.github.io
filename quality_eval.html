Full Instructions     (Expand/Collapse)

Thanks for participating in this HIT! You will read a sentence fragment describing a belief someone reading about a news event would have. This belief either describes the intent of an author writing about the news event, how a reader would react to that event, or actions a reader would take after reading about the news event. Please rate the quality of the belief description based on the following questions:

  1. What is the overall quality of the belief description in terms of coherence and relevance?
  2. Does the belief convey feelings or thoughts that are (generally) considered socially acceptable?
  3. Do you think it is possible that the belief and/or news event perpetuate negative social biases or stereotypes?
  4. Does the belief description impact whether or not you think the news event is trustworthy?
