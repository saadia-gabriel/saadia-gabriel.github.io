Instructions

Read a headline from an ad and label (1) what you think the motivations of the ad are, and (2) what reactions someone might have to the ad.

Full Instructions     (Expand/Collapse)

Thanks for participating in this HIT! You will read a sentence fragment depicting a headline from an ad.

Think about what might be implied by the ad described, including the reaction it might invoke from someone reading it, whether it would be seen as appealing or manipulative, and what the intent\purpose of the ad is.

We ask the following questions:

1a. Does the ad affect prior beliefs? (for this, consider if you have prior beliefs about the content of the ad and whether seeing the ad either reinforces them or changes your mind)

1b. What is the intent/purpose of the ad? (many ads may be purposefully unclear or deceptive, try to determine the likely intent as well as you can. If you are absolutely unsure, you may skip this question.)

1c. How effective is the ad? (does it feel manipulative or do you think it is genuine in its presentation?)

1d. How interesting is the ad? (is this an ad you'd be interested in clicking on or sharing with others?)

1e. How relevant is the ad to your life (does the ad cover a topic you need to learn more about or present a product you'd use)?

Please answer as well as you can.
